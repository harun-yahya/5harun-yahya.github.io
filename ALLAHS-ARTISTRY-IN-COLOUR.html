ALLAH'S ARTISTRY IN COLOUR

by ADNAN OKTAR (HARUN YAHYA)

October, 2003

File Size 5.9MB

READ DOWNLOAD

Have you ever thought what it would be like to live in a world without colour? Free yourself for a moment from your experience, forget all that you've learned and start using your imagination. Try to visualise your body, the people around you, the seas, the sky, trees, flowers, in short everything in black. Imagine that there is no colour around you. Try to think how you would feel if people, cats, dogs, birds, but terflies, and fruits had no colour at all. You would never want to live in such a world, would you?
